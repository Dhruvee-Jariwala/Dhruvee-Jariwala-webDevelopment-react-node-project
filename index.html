About Dream City Group

Creating marvelous spaces
Since 2008

From the small team to the flourished group of experts, the Dream City group has come a long way all because of its dedication, consistency, commitment, and customer satisfaction.

To take the development and construction industry to new heights Dream City group came into action in 2008. The foundation of the Dream City group emerged from Olpad, Surat which was later shifted to Bharuch.
