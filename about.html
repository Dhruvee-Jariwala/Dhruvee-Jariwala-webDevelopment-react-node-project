About Dream City Group

Creating marvelous spaces
Since 2008

From the small team to the flourished group of experts, the Dream City group has come a long way all because of its dedication, consistency, commitment, and customer satisfaction.

To take the development and construction industry to new heights Dream City group came into action in 2008. The foundation of the Dream City group emerged from Olpad, Surat which was later shifted to Bharuch.

What Dream City Group has for you?

Well,Dream City has everything to make you feel WOW! Fitness, Garden, Entertainment, Fun, Learning! Dream City has room for all your needs. Amazing! Right? But you know what's even more interesting, you get everything at Dream City at budget-friendly prices with the best services just at your fingertips.

One of our major developments is the Dream City business hub located in Bharuch. It's one of the ultimate epitomes of our hard work and creativity.
